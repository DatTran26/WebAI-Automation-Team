Experience the soulful taste of Hanoi in your own kitchen. Our Phở Broth Concentrate is slow-simmered for 24 hours using premium beef bones, charred ginger, star anise, and cinnamon. No MSG, no preservatives—just pure, concentrated flavor that honors tradition.
