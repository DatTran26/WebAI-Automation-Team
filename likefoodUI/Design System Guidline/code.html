LIKEFOOD Design System & Brand Guidelines

Brand Philosophy

"Modern High-Contrast Editorial - Where Heritage meets Professionalism"

The LIKEFOOD identity is built on the intersection of culinary tradition and digital precision. We use bold typography and a rich, heritage-inspired palette to create an experience that feels both authoritative and appetizing.
