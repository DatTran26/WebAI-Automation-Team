# Project Overview & PDR (Product Development Requirements)

## 1. Project Overview

**Project Name:** LIKEFOOD
**Domain:** E-commerce, food retail, digital platform
**Model:** Omnichannel (combining offline operations with online sales)
**Target Market:** United States, focusing on the Vietnamese community
**Deployment Direction:** Website is the central sales channel; Facebook and TikTok are used for promotion, traffic acquisition, and communication support.

LIKEFOOD is a specialized platform for selling Vietnamese specialty foods to customers in the U.S. It centers around a modern online shopping experience, integrating live commerce, automated payments, order management, and AI-powered product assistance. It aims to evolve into a production-ready business system.

## 2. Background and Problem Statement

The Vietnamese community in the U.S. has strong demand for traditional specialty foods (traditional cakes, fermented fish sauces, dried foods, candies, nuts, tea, etc.). Current problems include:
- Hard-to-access authentic Vietnamese flavors.
- Lack of a trusted brand with clear info and professional presentation.
- Heavy reliance on offline channels or general social media.
- No specialized digital platform focusing on both shopping experience and online conversion.
- The direct on-website livestream selling model is underutilized.

## 3. Project Goals

### 3.1 Short-Term Goals
- Build a complete MVP.
- Demonstrate real live commerce features directly on the site.
- Implement automated payments via Stripe.
- Prove cloud/VPS deployment capabilities.
- Integrate basic AI to enhance value and assistance.

### 3.2 Mid-Term Goals
- Standardize the online sales model for the business.
- Connect existing offline sales activities with the online system.
- Expand reach via website, Facebook, and TikTok.

### 3.3 Long-Term Goals
- Grow LIKEFOOD into a recognized Vietnamese specialty platform in the U.S.
- Expand AI, marketing automation, and recommendation engine.
- Upgrade infrastructure to handle production scale.

## 4. Product Scope

Focuses on Vietnamese specialty product groups:
- Cakes & Pastries
- Fish Sauces & Pastes
- Dried Foods (fish, shrimp, squid)
- Candies & Sweets
- Nuts & Snacks
- Related Specialties (tea, jam, gift items)

## 5. Target Audience

- **Primary Audience:** Vietnamese people residing in the U.S., aged 25–55.
- **Needs:** Seeking authentic Vietnamese specialties for personal use/gifting, preferring online shopping with domestic U.S. delivery. They value trustworthy brands, clarity, livestream sales, flash deals, fast checkout, and guidance.

## 6. Core Value Propositions

1. **Trust:** Clear origin, professional presentation, secure payment.
2. **Convenience:** Fast shopping, mobile-first, livestream purchasing.
3. **Cultural Emotion:** Evoking warmth, nostalgia, and home.
4. **Conversion:** Pinned products, flash deals, smooth cart/checkout.

## 7. Business Model

- **Type:** B2C (Business to Consumer).
- **Channels:** E-commerce website (primary), Facebook & TikTok (marketing).
- **Strategy:** Livestreams hosted directly on the site; payment fully automated via Stripe.

## 8. Definition of MVP (Stage 2)

A fully working system capable of:
- Searching/browsing products and categories.
- Streaming live sessions on the website with real-time pinned products and cart addition.
- Checking out with automated Stripe payments handling status updates to PAID.
- Acting as an AI product assistant (chatbot).
- A basic admin control panel for live stream and product control.
- Designed with strong foundations to continue scaling post-competition.
